## Article 1 (Purpose)

These Terms set out the rights, obligations, and responsibilities between TerraWorld (the "Company") and its members regarding the use of the daily-record gamified wellness service (the "Service") provided by the Company.

## Article 2 (Definitions)

1. "Service": the service through which a member records daily activities via the web (`https://terraworld.app`) and the Android/iOS apps, earns virtual goods (Dew, Sunshine, category tokens), and decorates a terrarium.
2. "Member": a person who has agreed to these Terms and entered into a use agreement with the Company.
3. "Virtual goods": non-cash assets (Dew, Sunshine, category tokens) earned through in-service activity or payment.
4. "Content": daily records, memos, photos, terrarium arrangements, etc. that a member posts or uploads to the Service.

1. The Company may amend these Terms within the bounds of applicable laws, including the Act on the Regulation of Terms and Conditions and the Network Act.
2. Amendments are announced via in-service notice and push notification at least 7 days before they take effect.
3. A member who does not agree to amended Terms may withdraw membership.

1. The use agreement is formed when a member agrees to these Terms, applies for membership, and the Company accepts.
2. The Company may refuse or subsequently cancel registration in the following cases:

1. Members must comply with these Terms, applicable laws, and the Company's operating policies.

1. The Company provides:
